Barcelona Modernism Tour

Tour Times and Cost


The tour leaves at 10:00 on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday from outside the bar


The cost of the tour is 15 euros per person


Learn about the magnificent works of Catalan Architect, Gaudí and marvel at the likes of the, yet, uncompleted La Sagrada Familia (undoubtedly, his most famous piece of work).
You’ll also get to visit other, equally important Modernist buildings in the Eixample neighbourhood of Barcelona from the mid-19th century during this 3 hour tour.

What you’ll experience:

  • Passeig de Gracia, the city’s premier shopping and living street that includes amazing architecture designed by Gaudí
  • Casa Calvet and Casa Batlló designed by Gaudí
  • Basilica de la Sagrada Familia, UNESCO World Heritage Site, a Roman Catholic church, also designed by Gaudí
  • Buildings designed by 19th Century architects, Josep Puig i Cadafalch, Salvador Valeri i Pupurull and Josep Puig i Cadafalch
