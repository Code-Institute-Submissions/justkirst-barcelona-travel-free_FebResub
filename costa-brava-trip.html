Day Trip to Costa Brava

Back to Tours Page

Tour Times and Cost


The tour leaves every Monday, Wednesday and Saturday at 09:00

The cost of the tour is 35 euros per person



Full day exploring the Costa Brava region with stops in some stunning towns along the way.
Weather permitting, there will be time for some swimming, sunbathing or even water sports if that sort of thing tickles your fancy.

What you’ll experience:

  • Private air-conditioned transport (maximum 8 people)
  • Visit – Lloret de Mar, Roses and Cadaqués
  • A 3 course lunch in a seaside restaurant (one of our faves)
