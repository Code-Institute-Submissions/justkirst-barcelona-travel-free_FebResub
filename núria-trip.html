2 Day Trip to Vall de Núria

Back to Tours Page

Tour Times and Cost


The tour leaves every Friday at 10:30 from outside the bar


The cost of the tour is 350 euros per person



Explore the Catalan Pyrenees in this 2 day, 2 night trip to Vall de Núria. You’ll stay at the Mountain Resort on a half-board basis. The resort is situated amongst peaks of almost 3000 meters.
You’ll be led by an expert guide for a Saturday morning hike and in the afternoon, during your free time, you’ll be able to play crazy golf, have a horse trek or take the cable car up to the top of the mountain for a birds-eye view of your surroundings.


What's included:

2 nights half-board accommodation in a double room
Guided hike around Vall de Núria
Guided tour and history of the church situated within the resort grounds
Private transport to Ribes de Fraser where you’ll catch the Rack Railway to the resort gates
Return ticket for the Rack Railway
