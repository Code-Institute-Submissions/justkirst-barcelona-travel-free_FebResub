Foodie Trip

Tour Times and Cost


The tour leaves at 18:30 every day, except from Sunday from outside the bar


The cost of the tour is 45 euros per person



Barcelona Travel Free’s Foodie tour will show you 3 restaurants loved by locals. And, for good reason too. They are authentic and with high quality food and drink to keep you feasted for the duration of your time with us.

What you’ll experience:


  • 3 restaurants
  • Different dishes in each restaurant (tapas, main dishes and deserts)
  • Drinks in each restaurant we visit
  • A local, knowledgeable guide who will give all the details of the history, culture and local life of Barcelona
