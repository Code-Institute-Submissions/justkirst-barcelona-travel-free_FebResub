Day Trip to Montserrat

Back to Tours Page

Tour Times and Cost


The tour leaves on Saturday at 08:30 from outside the bar


The cost of the tour is 30 euros per person and includes all transport



Explore one of Catalunya’s cultural highlights – Montserrat Mountain and Monastery.

You’ll be led by one of our guides throughout. In your private transport there, the guide will tell you about Catalan culture, and other local tip-bits of knowledge to keep you entertained on your journey. Your guide will also give a tour of the Basilica and Monastery before giving you some free time to explore the area.

What you’ll experience:

  • Visit the sacred rock of Montserrat
  • Experience breathtaking panoramic views of the Montserrat region
  • Private tour of the Basilica and Monastery
