Getting to Barcelona

By Plane



Barcelona airport, also known as Josep Tarradellas Barcelona-El Prat, is located 16km south of the city.
Barcelona City Centre can be reached easily by metro, bus and train to all major stations. A good option is to use the direct bus called, ‘The Aerobús’. It stops at 4 major points in the city and takes 35 minutes to reach the final stop, Plaça Catalunya.
You can buy tickets in advance, directly from their website.

By Train



Barcelona operates a large number of high-speed rail routes from France and other parts of Spain.
As an example, it takes just 6 hours from Paris Gare de Lyon station to Barcelona Sants Station in a comfortable seat. It’s environmentally friendly and a way to see some beautiful French and Spanish countryside on your journey to the Catalan Capital.
You can book trains directly from either Oui.SNCF for your Paris-Barcelona journey or Renfe for journeys around Spain.
